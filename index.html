Real-time updates in one spot, for all of your mining rigs

cryptoGlance is an open-source, self-hosted PHP webapp providing you with a glance at all of your crypto-currency components in one spot.

Coming Soonwe're almost ready! Installation Guidealso coming soon...

Installation is easy. If you've ever setup websites on a server, you'll be up and running in a few minutes. On the other hand, if you don't have that experience under your belt, don't fret. A simple-to-follow installation guide is just above, how convenient!

Features

Overview

At one glance, see a concise listing of your rigs, and their current performance + health status.

Balance

Keep an eye on the balance of your addresses, as they're verified against the blockchain.

Rigs.

Each rig shows a summary which can easy be expanded upon via tabs. Detailed status of each device/GPU shows you everything.
We're in the early, yet release-worthy stages of cryptoGlance development, so you can be sure that new features will be added as time goes on.
